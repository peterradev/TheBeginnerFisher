Welcome To The Beginner Fisherman

This website is for the people who are learning how to bass fish.

Baits

There are many different baits that you can use to catch fish.

  • Soft Baits (Also referred to as plastics)
  • Hardbaits
  • Swimbaits
  • Jigs
  • Frogs
  • Spinnerbaits
  • Buzzbaits

There will be some baits that might cross over with other types of baits

Seasons

As the seasons come and go, the temperature will change. With that some baits will work better than all the other baits. These are seasons that will go into greater detail on this site:

  • Spring (Pre-Spawn)
  • Spring (Spawn)
  • Spring (Post-Spawn)
  • Summer
  • Fall
